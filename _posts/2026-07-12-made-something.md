Spent the evening making a thing instead of scrolling past other people's things.
It came out lopsided. Doesn't matter. My hands knew a little more at the end than
they did at the start, and that's the whole point.

If you've been meaning to start something — this is me telling you it's allowed to
be bad first. That's just what the beginning looks like.
