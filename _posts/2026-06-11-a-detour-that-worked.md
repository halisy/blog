We missed the exit. Argued about it for a minute. Then the road bent around a hill
and there was this — gold everywhere, not a single other car.

Half the good things this year came from a wrong turn I was annoyed about at the time.
I'm starting to think that's not a coincidence.

![the light kept changing every few minutes](/assets/images/placeholder-3.svg)

Note to self: leave earlier, plan less.
